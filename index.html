Project Brief

1. The client specifically requested that I design a single symbol that was iconic enough to stand alone as the logo but also legible enough to read as the brand name. 2. I needed to incorporate a visual connection to the brand concept: Noah’s Ark.

Minimalism, luxury, and neo-modern were keywords for the feel of this brand. I needed to emphasize sophistication and high-quality while not communicating 'old' or 'traditional': as the product was to be mid-priced, timeless clothing pieces for young people.

Font: 'Big Caslon' was used as the "base" for creating this custom text.

To make the logo feel cohesive and united - each part belonging to the whole, elements were repeated throughout. Such as: line weight, curve radius, and serif style.
